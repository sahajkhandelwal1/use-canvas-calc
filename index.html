Features

Everything you need to
understand your grades.

Real-time Grade Tracking

See your current grade across all courses, updated live from Canvas. No manual entry ever.

What-If Grade Modeling

Simulate grade changes before they happen. Ask "what if I got 90 on the final?" and see it instantly.

Drag-and-Drop Reordering

Arrange your course cards in the order that matters to you. Changes persist across sessions.

Upcoming & Overdue

Never miss a deadline. See what's due soon and what's already late — all in one panel.

Custom Course Aliases

Rename "CHEM 101 Sec 4 — Spring 2025" to just "Chemistry." Your dashboard, your rules.

Background Customization

Choose from 8 preset backgrounds or upload your own. Make the dashboard feel like yours.

How It Works

Up and running
in 3 steps.

01

Get your API token

In Canvas: Account → Settings → Approved Integrations → New Access Token. Takes 30 seconds. No password sharing.

02

Enter your school domain

Type your Canvas URL — something like myschool.instructure.com. That's all we need.

03

See everything

Your grades, assignments, and projections load instantly. Start modeling what-ifs right away.
